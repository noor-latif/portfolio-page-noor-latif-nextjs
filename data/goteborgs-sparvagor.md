# Göteborgs Spårvägar AB - Technician & Programmer

**Duration:** September 2022 - February 2023 (6 months)
**Location:** Gothenburg, Västra Götaland County, Sweden

## Overview
Maintained IT infrastructure for Gothenburg's tram network, integrating embedded systems and IoT devices. Worked with vehicle computers, surveillance systems, and industrial control systems in a live operational transport environment.

## Key Responsibilities

### Critical Infrastructure Maintenance
- **Västtrafik Traffic Control Center (Trafikledningscentral):**
  - Serviced IT datacenter supporting critical traffic control operations
  - Ensured high availability and reliability of control systems
  - Performed preventive maintenance and emergency repairs
  - Maintained backup systems and disaster recovery infrastructure

- **Vehicle Computer Systems:**
  - Serviced and maintained onboard computers in tram fleet
  - Troubleshot hardware and software issues in operational vehicles
  - Ensured minimal downtime for public transportation services
  - Coordinated maintenance schedules with operational requirements

- **Network Equipment:**
  - Configured and maintained network infrastructure across tram network
  - Ensured reliable connectivity for real-time systems
  - Implemented network security measures
  - Monitored network performance and resolved connectivity issues

- **Video Surveillance Systems:**
  - Maintained video surveillance equipment for safety and security
  - Ensured recording systems were operational and compliant
  - Troubleshot camera and recording issues
  - Managed storage and archival of surveillance footage

- **Programmable Logic Controllers (PLCs):**
  - Worked with internal PLCs controlling various tram systems
  - Performed diagnostics and troubleshooting of control systems
  - Ensured safety-critical systems remained operational
  - Coordinated with engineering teams on PLC programming updates

### Automation & Scripting
- **Firmware Provisioning Automation:**
  - Automated firmware deployment scripts for live travel display systems
  - Eliminated manual firmware update processes
  - Ensured consistent firmware versions across fleet
  - Reduced deployment time and human error
  - Created rollback procedures for failed updates

- **Deployment Automation:**
  - Automated deployment processes for critical transport infrastructure
  - Prototyped robot automation scripts to modernize legacy operational processes
  - Developed scripts for bulk configuration updates
  - Implemented validation checks for automated deployments
  - Created logging and monitoring for automated processes

### Industrial IoT Experience
- Worked hands-on with Industrial IoT systems in live operational transport environment
- Gained experience with real-time systems and high-availability requirements
- Understood the critical nature of public transportation infrastructure
- Learned to balance maintenance needs with operational continuity
